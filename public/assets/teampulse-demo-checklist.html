TeamPulse Demo Checklist

1. Pre-demo Setup

  • Verify the Teams app manifest includes the production content URL and valid domains (*.cloud.microsoft).
  • Confirm the admin tab renders within Teams web, Outlook web, and the Microsoft 365 hub.
  • Create one sample survey with responses to showcase analytics.

2. Live Demo Flow

  • Show the personal tab and highlight dashboard KPIs.
  • Create a new survey from the admin panel and demonstrate template editing.
  • Switch to Teams chat to capture real-time responses via the TeamPulse bot.
  • Display analytics and export options from the results modal.

3. Wrap-up

  • Review licensing tiers (Free, Pro, Enterprise) and upgrade paths.
  • Share the migration checklist for existing Pulse tools.
  • Invite the customer to book onboarding using success@incumate.io.
