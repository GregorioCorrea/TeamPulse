Logo Usage Guidelines
---------------------

Primary logo: wordmark "TeamPulse" with pulse icon.

Safe area: maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter "T" around the logo.

Backgrounds:
- Light mode: use the full-color logo. Hex colors #0B5CFF and #764BA2.
- Dark mode: use the white variant with 80% opacity shadow.

Minimum size: 120 px width for digital.

Do not rotate, stretch, or recolor the logomark outside the defined palette.
